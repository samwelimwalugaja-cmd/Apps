Welcome to HOCET YOUNG CHARITY GENERATION (HEYCG)

HEYCG is a youth-led charity organization dedicated to empowering young people and transforming communities through sustainable charity initiatives. Our membership management system allows members to pay fees, track contributions, participate in events, and make donations.

Founded with the vision of creating positive change, HEYCG has grown into a vibrant community of young changemakers committed to education support, healthcare assistance, and community outreach programs across Tanzania.

Our mission is to create lasting impact through collective action and dedicated service to those in need.

  • Support education for underprivileged children through scholarships and school supplies.
  • Organize community outreach programs and charity events throughout Tanzania.
  • Provide healthcare assistance and medical support to vulnerable communities.

Through our comprehensive member management system, members can easily pay membership fees, view their payment history, stay updated on upcoming events, access posters and announcements, and make donations to support our causes.

Complete Member Management for HEYCG

Our system allows admin, sub-admin, and members to manage all charity activities efficiently.

  • Easy online registration with admin approval workflow
  • Role-based access for Admin, Sub-admin, and Members..
  • Complete member profile management with education and job information

Admin has overall control including member approval, payment confirmation, event management, and report generation. Sub-admin assists with limited privileges.

Secure Payment Processing via Airtel Lipa Namba

Members can pay registration fee (TZS 3,000) and monthly membership fees (TZS 2,000) securely.

Payment is made through Airtel Lipa Namba number: 14493258 - YOUNG CHARITY GENERATION

  • Upload payment receipt for admin verification
  • View complete payment history and download receipts
  • 14-day payment deadline after registration
